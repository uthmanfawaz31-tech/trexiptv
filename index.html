Trex IPTV FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions.

Do I need special hardware?

No. Trex IPTV works on Smart TVs, Android, iOS, Firestick, MAG, PC and many more devices. If you can install an app or use a browser, you can watch.

Will my internet speed be enough?

In most cases, a stable 15–20 Mbps connection is more than enough for smooth HD streaming. For 4K and 8K, we recommend a higher speed and wired connection when possible.

How do I get support if I’m stuck?

You can contact our team on WhatsApp and we'll walk you through the setup or help you troubleshoot in just a few minutes.
